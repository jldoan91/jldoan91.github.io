About Linus Torvalds

  • His interest in computers began with the Commodore VIC-20
  • He wrote a Pac-Man clone named Cool Man
  • He originally wanted to name the kernel Freax but the manager of the ftp server it was stored on labeled his folder linux for Linus and the x for connection to unix
  • in 1999 both Red Hat and VA Linux presented Torvalds stock options in gratitude of his creation
  • In 2010 he became a United States citizen and registered to vote
  • He is married to Tove Tovalds, a sixtime Finnish national karate champion
  • In 2008 and 2012 he has stated and confirmed that he uses the Fedora distribution of Linux
  • Currently the Linux Foundation sponsors Torvalds so he can work full time on improving Linux
  • As of 2006 around 2% of the kernel was written by him
  • He stated that in 2012 his own contributions to the kernel are mostly merging code written by others
  • He holds the "Linux" trademark and retains the highest authority to decide what new code is implemeted in the standard Linux kernel
